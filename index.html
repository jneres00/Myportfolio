Graphic Design • Social Media

Hi, I'm Jessica

I’m a Graphic Design student from Brazil with a genuine passion for learning and improving a little more every day. I currently study at Uninter University and work with tools like Canva, Adobe Illustrator, and Figma to create visual solutions that are beautiful, functional, and meaningful.

In addition to design, I’ve been developing my skills in social media, including content creation, visual storytelling, and basic video editing using CapCut. I enjoy creating layouts, experimenting with styles, and crafting designs that communicate clearly and connect with audiences.

I also have basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which I occasionally use in small personal projects — mostly to explore simple visual or interactive ideas.

Beyond design, I study English, French, and Spanish independently because I believe that understanding other cultures expands creativity. I’m building my path with patience, curiosity, and a strong desire to create work that truly makes a difference.

Projects

A selection of academic, freelance, and personal projects developed using Canva, Illustrator, and other tools. Includes branding work, social media design, and visual compositions created for different contexts and needs.

Asymmetrical Composition – Academic Assignment
Piece developed as an exercise in asymmetrical composition, exploring contrast, visual hierarchy, and dynamic balance. The design combines organic typography with photographic elements of strawberries and ice cream, creating a vibrant and appetizing aesthetic. The layout highlights the product attractively while maintaining lightness and visual flow.

VANS Visual Identity Redesign
Academic project developed through research on the brand, its audience, and cultural references in skateboarding, graffiti, and streetwear. All logo concepts were initially sketched by hand and later digitized in Clip Studio Paint, where I refined the strokes and shapes to explore different visual styles. The final proposals reflect freedom, attitude, and authenticity — aligned with Vans’ urban spirit.

Café do Brejo Poster
Promotional poster created for the Café do Brejo brazilian brand during the “Graphic Design Journey” workshop by EBAC. The piece draws inspiration from Northeastern Brazilian woodcut printing, combining original illustrations, earthy tones, and cultural elements from the sertão. The design highlights the brand’s regional identity, emphasizing nature and tradition. The result is a harmonious poster with an artisanal feel, conveying the concept of specialty coffee produced in Pernambuco.

Nail Design Course Promotion
The project focuses on visual hierarchy, typography, and call-to-action design to promote a beginner-friendly course, using a soft pastel color palette and beauty-focused imagery to create a clear, professional, and engaging layout.

Horror Movie Cover
Complete DVD cover development for a horror title, including original artwork creation, typography choices, visual composition, and image editing. I also produced a realistic crime-scene mockup to present the design in a cinematic and professional context.

Pet Shop Advertisement
The layout features a playful background with paw-print patterns, friendly mascots, and rounded typography to convey warmth and trust. Includes a slogan and social media callouts to ensure clear and effective communication.
